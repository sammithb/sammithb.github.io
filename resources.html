What is this page about?

One of the most effective study habits I have developed in mathematics is creating resources and teaching the material to others. I highly recommend this to anybody looking to study mathematics as well.

As Aristotle said, “Those who know, do. Those who understand, teach.”

I share notes, guides, and some problems that I have created to prepare myself for specific courses. If you find any typos, please feel free to reach out to me.
